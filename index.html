Your Data, Your Control

Expense Manager stores all your expense data securely on your device. Google Drive access is only used when you manually choose to back up your data from the app’s Settings page. This allows you to save a personal copy of your records on your own Google Drive for safekeeping or transfer. The app does not automatically upload, access, or modify any other files in your Drive. You remain in full control — backups are created and managed only when you choose to do so.
