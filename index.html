Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Expense Manager

Is Expense Manager completely free?

Yes! Expense Manager is 100% free with no subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no ads. All features including Privacy Mode, Smart Reminders, Multi-Currency Support, Budget Tracking, and Google Drive Backup are available to all users at no cost.

How does Privacy Mode protect my financial data?

Privacy Mode hides all monetary amounts and receipt images throughout the app with a single toggle. Perfect for checking your finances in public places like cafes, trains, or offices without revealing sensitive information. You can enable it from Settings > Security > Privacy Mode.

Which currencies are supported?

Expense Manager supports 35+ global currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, JPY, AUD, CAD, CNY, and many more. You can select your preferred currency during setup or change it anytime from Settings > Preferences > Currency. Perfect for travelers and expats!

Is my data stored on your servers?

No! All your financial data is stored locally on your device only. We never upload your transaction data to any external servers. Google Drive access is only used when you choose to create backups - either manually or via scheduled auto-backup. You remain in full control with no tracking, no ads, and no data selling.

How do Smart Reminders work?

Smart Reminders help you never miss a bill or recurring expense. Set reminders 1, 3, or 7 days before your recurring transactions are due. The app automatically reschedules reminders daily for reliability and works across device reboots and timezone changes. Manage all reminders from the dedicated Reminders screen.

Can I track both income and expenses?

Absolutely! Expense Manager lets you track both income and expenses in one place. See your true net balance with dedicated categories for salary, freelance income, investments, as well as bills, shopping, food, and more. The Unified Analysis Dashboard shows comprehensive insights for both income and expense trends.

How does Budget Tracking help me?

Budget Tracking lets you set monthly or yearly spending limits by category or overall. Get smart 4-tier alerts (Healthy, Warning, Critical, Over Budget) as you approach your limits. The app helps you stay within budget with visual progress indicators and detailed spending breakdowns by category.

What devices are supported?

Expense Manager is available for Android devices running Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher. The app is optimized for phones and tablets with support for both portrait and landscape orientations. Download now from Google Play Store!
