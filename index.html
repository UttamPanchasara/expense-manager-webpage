A Private, Offline Expense Tracker — Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

Unlike most finance apps, Expense Manager stores all your financial data securely on your device — no cloud sync, no external servers. Google Drive access is only used when you choose to back up your data — either manually or via scheduled auto-backup. The app does not access or modify any other files in your Drive. Your receipts and attachments are synced only to your personal backup folder. No tracking. No data selling. No accounts required. Just a private, offline money manager built for people who value their financial privacy.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Expense Manager

Everything you need to know about this free offline expense tracker & budget planner app

Is Expense Manager free to use?

Yes! Expense Manager is free to download and use. Core features like Privacy Mode, Smart Reminders, Multi-Currency Support, Budget Tracking, Savings Goals, and Google Drive Backup are available to all users. Premium features like Multiple Profiles and Payment Mode Analytics are available with a premium upgrade.

How do Multiple Profiles work?

Multiple Profiles is a premium feature that lets you separate your finances into dedicated spaces — for example, Personal, Business, and Family. Each profile has its own transactions, budgets, savings goals, categories, and reports. Switch between profiles instantly from Settings. All data stays on your device.

What is Payment Mode Analytics?

Payment Mode Analytics is a premium feature that shows how you spend across UPI, Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, and Wallet. View donut charts for mode distribution, 6-month trends, and smart insights like "UPI is your top mode (45% of spend)". Available in monthly, yearly, and all-time views in the Analysis Dashboard.

How does Privacy Mode protect my financial data?

Privacy Mode hides all monetary amounts and receipt images throughout the app with a single toggle. Perfect for checking your finances in public places like cafes, trains, or offices without revealing sensitive information. You can enable it from Settings > Security > Privacy Mode.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No! All your financial data is stored locally on your device only. We never upload your transaction data to any external servers. Google Drive access is only used when you choose to create backups — either manually or via scheduled auto-backup. You remain in full control with no tracking and no data selling.

Can I track both income and expenses?

Absolutely! Expense Manager lets you track both income and expenses in one place. See your true net balance with dedicated categories for salary, freelance income, investments, as well as bills, shopping, food, and more. The Analysis Dashboard shows comprehensive insights with category breakdowns. Premium users can also track across multiple profiles and view payment mode analytics.

How does Budget Tracking help me?

Budget Tracking lets you set monthly or yearly spending limits by category or overall. Get smart 4-tier alerts (Healthy, Warning, Critical, Over Budget) as you approach your limits. The app helps you stay within budget with visual progress indicators and detailed spending breakdowns by category.

What devices are supported?

Expense Manager is available for Android devices running Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher. The app is optimized for phones and tablets with support for both portrait and landscape orientations. Download now from Google Play Store!

Is Expense Manager completely free to download?

Yes. Expense Manager is free to download and use. Core features — expense tracking, budget planning, savings goals, privacy mode, multi-currency support, and Google Drive backup — are all available at no cost. A one-time premium upgrade unlocks advanced features like multiple profiles and payment mode analytics.

Can I track expenses without an internet connection?

Absolutely. Expense Manager is a fully offline expense tracker — all your financial data lives on your device, never a server. You can add transactions, view reports, manage budgets, and track savings goals with no internet. Google Drive backup is optional and only runs when you trigger it. No login required, no forced cloud sync, no tracking.

How do I track my daily expenses on Android?

Download Expense Manager from Google Play — it's free. Tap the + button to log any expense or income in seconds. Choose a category (Food, Transport, Shopping, etc.), link it to an account (Cash, Bank, Card), and optionally attach a receipt photo. Your dashboard instantly updates your balance, budget status, and monthly summary. Set up recurring transactions for fixed bills so they log automatically.

How does Multi-Account Tracking work?

Multi-Account Tracking lets you create and manage multiple accounts — Cash, Bank, Credit Card, Savings, and more — all in one app. Every transaction is linked to a specific account so you always know exactly where your money is coming from and going to. You can view per-account balance summaries and filter transactions by account. A default Cash account is created automatically when you set up your profile.

What is the Home Screen Widget?

The Home Screen Widget is a 4×3 Android widget that displays a GitHub-style 16-week activity heatmap of your spending habits directly on your home screen. It shows this month's income and expense amounts, your active days count, and current streak — without opening the app. The widget syncs automatically every time you add a transaction or launch the app. Tap anywhere on it to open Expense Manager instantly.
