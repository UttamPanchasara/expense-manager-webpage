Can I backup my data without cloud sync?

Yes! Expense Manager offers optional Google Drive backup that you control. Your data is encrypted and only accessible by you. You can also export to PDF or CSV for local backups.

Will offline apps work on all Android devices?

Most offline expense trackers work on Android 5.0 and above. They require minimal storage (typically 50-100MB) and don't need constant internet access.

How secure is my data in an offline app?

Offline apps are generally more secure since your data never leaves your device. Look for apps with biometric lock (fingerprint/face ID) and privacy mode for additional security layers.
